Frequently asked questions.

Use the calculator as a starting point. A Sighthound engineer can help you tune hardware mixes, validate performance, and refine your total cost of ownership model for each site.

How accurate is the calculator?

The calculator reflects current list pricing for compute nodes and cameras, including the 5% bulk discount rule. Taxes, shipping, and optional support plans are not included and will be added to a formal quote.

Can I change discounts or add other hardware?

Yes. For large or multi-region rollouts we often layer in custom incentives or additional SKUs. Use the calculator to frame the order of magnitude, then request a quote so we can model your exact mix.

Do I need cloud connectivity?

No. The stack is designed for on-premise video analytics and edge AI hardware. You can run real-time computer vision entirely on-site and choose how and when to sync to the cloud.

What if my deployment grows over time?

The same Sighthound hardware pricing and bulk discount rules apply as you scale. Many customers start with a pilot, then expand to full, scalable deployment once performance and economics are proven.

Ready to model your next site?

Share your camera counts, retention targets, and latency requirements. We will validate a hardware design that hits both your accuracy goals and your budget, then lock in pricing for your rollout.
